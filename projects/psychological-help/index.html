About us

Our mission is to improve the mental health of people around the world and make psychotherapy a natural part of the culture of life

Talk was founded in February 2022, in order to provide high-quality and fast psychological help to people in different parts of the world. We ourselves have come across how difficult it is to find a good and experienced psychologist. We've done a ton of research to create a product that our customers really want. Carefully selected psychotherapists are already ready to work with you, and every day our technological solutions improve to provide you with the best service.Every day we work to break down prejudices about psychological help.

Someone believes that our services are only for the weak, and someone says that it is impossible to work online. We believe that this is not the case. That psychotherapy is not shameful, it is convenient, it is important. As with physical health care, mental health is about the small bricks with which we build the foundation of our happy life. Questions and answers

  • How to formulate a request to a psychologist?

    Identifying the root of the problem on your own can be quite difficult. Don't worry - a psychotherapist can help with making a request.

    In this case, you should describe the symptoms of the problem:

    • What exactly is bothering you at the moment?
    • How does it affect areas of your life?
    • What do you think this could be related to?
  • Which direction of psychotherapy is better to choose?

    The main directions have passed the test of time and have proven their effectiveness. Therefore, it would be incorrect to say that some of them are more effective, and some less so. Moreover, the specialist himself plays a big role in the effectiveness of therapy.

    We suggest using the following principles when choosing:

    • cognitive-behavioral therapy or schema therapy: if you have a clear request to work on a certain disorder (panic attacks, insomnia, etc.);
    • analytical methods (for example, transactional analysis): if there is a need to understand and realize unconscious processes, emotions, etc.;
    • Gestalt therapy: suitable for the needs of working with emotions and focusing and "here and now" as well as for working out interpersonal relationships;
    • working with a coach: will help define and/or achieve goals;
    • but our psychotherapists also work in other methods.
  • Is online psychotherapy effective?

    Scientists have proven that remote online therapy is not only not inferior in effectiveness to traditional face-to-face therapy, but in a number of cases surpasses it. After all, online psychotherapy is:

    • security;
    • comfort;
    • saving time and money on transport costs;
    • availability;
    • a large selection of specialists.
  • What result will I get?

    During psychotherapy you will receive:

    1. Professional support and help from a specialist in the process of "being yourself".
    2. The skill of doing what you want and not doing what you don't want.
    3. A clear awareness of what you like and what you don't like, and the opportunity to express it.
    4. Ability to build quality relationships.
    5. The ability to expand personal boundaries.
    6. The ability to solve difficult situations in one's life.
  • Why is psychotherapy absolutely normal?

    Therapy allows you to enjoy the emotional side of life. Just live. Qualitatively and fully. Regardless of the expectations of others. Without your goals and values.

    Psychotherapy is normal for a person who really wants to develop. It is a tool that allows you to gain resilience, independence and the freedom to be yourself.

    The only exception is if our specialist sees that a person may harm himself or others. In this case, he can contact the relevant institutions.
